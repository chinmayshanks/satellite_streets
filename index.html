Satellite / Streets

A comparison tool for checking OSM map completeness around the world.

Use the geosearch () to search for places around the world. Click anywhere on the map to get the coordinates of that point.

Coordinates:

This tool is meant to be a supporting tool to OSM mappers. You can go to any place around the world and compare the mapping progress for that region. Click on the map above to get the coordinates for that point. Import those coordinates to your favourite OSM editor and get started!
